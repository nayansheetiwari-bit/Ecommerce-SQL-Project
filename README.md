# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Insights Analysis using SQL

## 📌 Project Overview
In this project, I analyzed an **e-commerce sales dataset using MySQL** to understand business performance, customer purchasing behavior, product demand, and category-wise profitability.

The main objective of this project was to use **SQL as a business analysis tool**, not just for querying data but for solving real-world business questions such as identifying top customers, understanding repeat purchase behavior, ranking profitable products, and segmenting customers based on value.

This project combines **data modeling, joins, aggregations, business KPI analysis, window functions, and actionable business insights**.

The project was extended using **advanced SQL concepts** to simulate real analyst workflows:

- `RANK()` for customer ranking

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- electronics emerged as the **highest revenue-generating category**
- laptops, smartphones, and tablets contributed the **highest overall profit**
- a small group of customers generated a large share of revenue
- multiple customers showed **repeat purchase behavior**
- lower-value categories performed better in quantity than in revenue
- revenue spikes were mostly driven by premium product purchases

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- retain high-value customers using loyalty rewards
- promote premium electronics products for higher profitability
- use bundling strategies for low-performing categories
- target repeat buyers with personalized upselling offers
- monitor category-level performance for better inventory planning
