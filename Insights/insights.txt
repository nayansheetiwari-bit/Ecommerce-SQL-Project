E-COMMERCE SQL PROJECT – BUSINESS INSIGHTS

1. Overall Business Performance
The business generated strong revenue primarily driven by high-value electronics products such as laptops, smartphones, and tablets. Profit margins are healthy, indicating effective pricing and cost management strategies.

2. Customer Behavior
Customers show active purchasing behavior, with several customers placing multiple orders. This indicates early signs of customer retention and engagement.

3. High-Value Customers
A small group of customers contributes a large portion of total revenue. These high-value customers can be targeted with loyalty programs, exclusive discounts, and personalized recommendations.

4. Product Performance
Electronics products generate the highest revenue, while categories like stationery and lifestyle contribute more in terms of quantity rather than revenue. This highlights a difference between high-volume and high-value products.

5. Category Insights
Electronics is the top-performing category in terms of both sales and profit. Furniture contributes moderate revenue, while accessories and stationery support consistent lower-value transactions.

6. Sales Trend
Sales are relatively stable across the observed period, with noticeable spikes on days when high-ticket items are purchased. This suggests revenue is influenced by fewer but high-value transactions.

7. Repeat Customers
Several customers have placed more than one order, indicating good repeat purchase behavior. This presents opportunities for upselling and cross-selling.

8. Profitability Analysis
Products like laptops, smartphones, and tablets generate the highest profits. Focusing marketing and inventory strategies on these products can maximize profitability.

9. Customer Segmentation
Customers can be segmented into High, Medium, and Low value groups based on their total spending. High-value customers contribute significantly to overall revenue and should be prioritized for retention strategies.

10. Growth Opportunities
Lower-performing categories such as lifestyle and stationery can be improved by bundling them with high-performing products or offering promotional discounts to increase their sales contribution.
